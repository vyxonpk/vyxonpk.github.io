FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is VYXON safe for septic tanks?

Yes. VYXON's formula is biodegradable and safe for use with standard septic systems when used as directed.

How long does the fragrance last?

The Alpine Blue fragrance system is designed to stay noticeable for up to 24 hours after each application.

Can I use VYXON on colored ceramic?

Yes. The pH-balanced formula is safe on all standard ceramic finishes, including colored and glazed surfaces.

How often should I use it?

For best results, apply 2-3 times per week. Daily use is safe for households with heavier traffic.

Where is VYXON manufactured?

VYXON is formulated and bottled in Pakistan under strict quality-control standards, batch-tested before distribution.
